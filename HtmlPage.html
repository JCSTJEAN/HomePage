About Me

WHO IS CURTIS ST- JEAN?

Curtis ST- JEAN (Curtis Angel) of Studio Ephrenia, is a multimedia artist specializing heavily in photography and videography as well as music. Since AngelJCProductions has been rebranded to Studio Ephrenia, he and his team and shifted focus from creating promotional videos to storytelling pieces.

How Do I Contact Him?

You can currently contact him at AngelJCProductions@gmail.com to book a performance or for other freelance commissions/jobs like producing an artwork, designs, paintings, etc.

Musical Journey

In 2012, Curtis Angel had released a self made acoustic EP doubling as a capstone and a side project. He named it "First Listen." It was released around May and was his first album ever. It had contained seven songs and was a reflection of his childish feelings and ideals he gained growing up. Although he was happy with the album, he felt as if he could do better and that the album wasn't a direct representation of who he was and who he was becoming. Curtis took a break to further develop different aspects of his musicianship and artistry.

In 2014, on february 14th, he released a single called "Lost In This World" as a fun video displaying the progress of his journey of discovering himself artistically, mentally and spiritually. During that time, he had also publicly shared his photography and other works online with others.

In 2015, on february 14th, Curtis Angel had shared another song, but this time an instrumental of a song with a new instrument he had picked up. By the end of March, Curtis Angel had released an official fullband song, The Getaway, to showcase his growth and to share some of his experiences during his time away.

2016-now, He is working on a few short films as well as producing and promoting for a new independant LOFI artist K E N K U R A.
